Jessica

Jess is not only the owner, but a stylist and colour & extensions specialist. She strives for the salon to provide a combination of brilliant customer service and outstanding treatments. To Jess, it's most important that each customer feels comfortable during their stay and feels like a changed person when they leave.

Carly

Carly is a woman of many talents. She is a nail & beauty therapist, a stylist, and a bonded extensions specialist. Carly has been at the salon from day one and although she has a great client base is always looking for new customers to help look their best.

Lucy

Lucy is the in-house eyebrow technician specialising in HD Brows. For those that aren't familiar with HD Brows, it's a unique treatment for eyebrows that requires your stylist to assess your face shape and colouring before creating a bespoke brow. Lucy has been creating perfectly well-groomed eyebrows as an HD Brows stylist for almost a year now and even has her own partitioned studio within the salon.
