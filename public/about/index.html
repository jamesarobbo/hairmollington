    About

    We're a boutique hair salon in Mollington, Chester. We love hair and we love beauty. We love it so much so that if we could be open twenty four hours a day we would be.

    At our salon, you can choose from a wide range of services that cover hair, nails and beauty. While you're with us we'll do our best to make you feel comfortable. We have hot drinks, cold drinks and snacks - the best bit is that they're all complimentary.

    After working for someone else for long enough, in 2013, Jess decided to venture out on her own. It wasn't long before she found the perfect location for her new salon in Mollington just outside of Chester, close enough to Chester so as to not be inconvenient but far enough away that it could create its own identity. The salon has been thriving ever since and strives to provide each customer with great, friendly service at reasonable prices.
